About Pastor Anil Andrewz

Early Life and Calling

Pastor Anil Andrewz is the founder of the Anil Andrewz Trust. He accepted the Lord as his personal Savior and was baptized on January 7th, 2001. Since then, he has been serving the Lord by preaching God's word through many organizations.

Full-Time Ministry

In 2012, while praying for a full-time ministry, he clearly heard God's voice saying, "Search and bring back my lost sheep." Obeying this call, he dedicated his life to full-time ministry. Since then, without compromise, he has been preaching the Gospel and God's word to strengthen many souls spiritually.

Rural Outreach and Church Planting

Following a vision from the Lord in 2019, Pastor Anil visited many rural villages and discovered that these areas lacked proper churches and pastors. With a burdened heart, he started encouraging pastors and became actively involved in church planting. His ministry also includes training youth as Sunday school teachers to ensure that children in these areas are saved.

Personal Life

According to God's will, he married Selvin Nissi on January 7th, 2020. Together, they are working in God's vineyard.
